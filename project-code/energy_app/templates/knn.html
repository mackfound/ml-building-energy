How KNN Works

          

            KNN finds the 15 most similar buildings in the training set based on
            Euclidean distance across all features, then predicts energy use as a
            distance-weighted average of those neighbors' actual consumption.
            Unlike Random Forest, it makes no assumptions about the shape of the relationship —
            it simply asks "what did buildings like yours actually use?"
