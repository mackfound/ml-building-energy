**AME34351 Group 09**

This repository is the codebase for Group 09 in the University of Notre Dame's AME34351 Course: Machine Learning for Engineers. Our goal 
with this project is to predict the energy consumption of residential buildings using (approximately) eight architectural/geometric inputs:
compactness, surface area, wall area, roof area, overall height, orientation, glazing area, and glazing area orientation. The goal is to 
minimize energy consumption for architects, engineers, and designers who want to create new and energy-efficient building designs.
